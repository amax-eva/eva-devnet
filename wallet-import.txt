1）助记词导入 保存在 --key-dir 目录中

deposit existing-mnemonic

deposit existing-mnemonic --validator_start_index=0 --num_validators=10 --folder=./keys-one --chain=mainnet

deposit existing-mnemonic --validator_start_index=11 --num_validators=10 --folder=./keys-two --chain=mainnet

2）--key-dir 转化为 --wallet-dir

validator accounts import --keys-dir=./keys-one/validator_keys --wallet-dir=./wallets-one

validator accounts import --keys-dir=./keys-two/validator_keys --wallet-dir=./wallets-two
